# Demo Video Script

Target length: 2 minutes.

## Scene 1: Opening

Show the command center.

Voiceover: "AssetIQ Brain is a unified asset and operations brain for industrial teams. It connects drawings, work orders, SOPs, inspections, manuals, and compliance records into one queryable intelligence layer."

## Scene 2: Ingestion

Click "Run demo ingestion" or open "Ingestion Pipeline".

Voiceover: "The ingestion pipeline parses heterogeneous records, extracts equipment tags and operational entities, and links them into an industrial knowledge graph."

## Scene 3: Knowledge Graph

Return to the command center and point to P-204 at the graph center.

Voiceover: "Here P-204 is linked to its P&ID, work order, OEM manual, inspection report, startup SOP, and regulatory evidence checklist."

## Scene 4: Copilot

Open "Expert Copilot" and ask: "Why is pump P-204 showing repeat seal failures?"

Voiceover: "The copilot gives a cited operational answer, not a generic response. It connects the clogged suction strainer, vibration spike, OEM limits, and startup procedure requirements."

## Scene 5: Maintenance RCA

Open "Maintenance RCA".

Voiceover: "The RCA agent turns cross-document evidence into field actions: clean the strainer, verify seal flush, monitor vibration, and schedule alignment checks."

## Scene 6: Compliance

Open "Compliance".

Voiceover: "The compliance agent flags missing PI-204 calibration evidence against OISD requirements and prepares the audit follow-up path."

## Close

Voiceover: "AssetIQ Brain reduces search time, protects retiring expert knowledge, and helps industrial teams make safer, faster, evidence-backed decisions."
